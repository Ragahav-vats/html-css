A synonym for "glimpse" is glance. Other options include peek, brief look, quick look, fleeting look, sight, and view. When used as a verb, "glimpse" can also be replaced with catch sight of, spot, spy, or espy.

A glimpse means a brief or momentary view. It implies seeing something for a very short time, not fully or completely. It can also refer to a vague or incomplete idea.
